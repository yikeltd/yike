FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
Yike is Nigeria's visual housing marketplace — built for renters and buyers who are tired of fake listings, scattered Facebook posts, and unverified agents.

Browse real homes with photos first, not paragraphs. Swipe through rentals, land, and shops like a feed. Search by city, area, and budget across Aba, Enugu, Owerri, Lagos, Abuja, and more.

WHY YIKE
• Visual-first browsing — see homes before you commit time
• Verified agents — identity-checked listers rank higher
• WhatsApp contact — chat agents directly in one tap
• Trust-first — report suspicious listings, read safety tips
• Free to browse — no login required to search or view listings

FOR AGENTS
List properties for free, get leads on WhatsApp, and build trust with verification.

Yike is a listing platform only. We do not collect rent, hold deposits, or guarantee properties. Always inspect in person before payment.
